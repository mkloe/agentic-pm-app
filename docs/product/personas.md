# User Personas

## Persona 1: Sarah — Team Lead PM
- **Role**: Product Manager at a 15-person startup
- **Experience**: 4 years in product, non-technical background
- **Goals**: Track team progress, prioritize backlog, report to stakeholders
- **Pain points**: Jira is too complex for her team size. Trello is too basic. Needs something in between.
- **Key behavior**: Checks the dashboard every morning. Moves tasks on the board during standup. Creates tasks from Slack conversations.
- **Success metric**: Can she get a project status overview in under 30 seconds?

## Persona 2: Marcus — Senior Developer
- **Role**: Frontend engineer, 6 years experience
- **Goals**: See what's assigned to him, update task status, avoid meetings about task status
- **Pain points**: Hates context-switching between code editor and project management tool. Wants to update status without leaving the terminal.
- **Key behavior**: Uses the board view. Drags tasks to "In Progress" at start of day, to "Done" when finished. Rarely opens backlog.
- **Success metric**: Can he update task status in under 5 seconds?

## Persona 3: Diana — Stakeholder (CEO)
- **Role**: Non-technical founder, checks in weekly
- **Goals**: Understand overall progress, identify blockers, see if the team is on track
- **Pain points**: Doesn't want to learn a tool. Wants someone to send her a summary.
- **Key behavior**: Receives a weekly dashboard screenshot or status email. Occasionally opens the dashboard link.
- **Success metric**: Can she understand project health from the dashboard without explanation?
